On Wednesday, October 16, at the Institut de France, at the Académie des beaux-arts, the juries of the international competition of the Fondation Jacques Rougerie gathered to award the Architecture and Innovation Prizes.

This 2024 edition marks a significant milestone in recognizing emerging talents through their visionary projects.

Intense deliberations were held by three exceptional juries. The nominated candidates in the three categories—rising sea levels due to climate change, the sea, and space—proposed extremely innovative solutions to address the major challenges of our time.

We would like to express our deep gratitude to all the candidates from five continents who, with passion and boldness, brought their projects to life by combining art, science, and technology with nature.

Their creativity and commitment to a sustainable and desirable future are a source of inspiration for us all.

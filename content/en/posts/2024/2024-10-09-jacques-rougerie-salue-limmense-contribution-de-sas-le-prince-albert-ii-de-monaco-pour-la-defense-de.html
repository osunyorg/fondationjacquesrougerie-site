During a moving ceremony, Jacques Rougerie delivered a heartfelt tribute to His Serene Highness Prince Albert II, Honorary President of the Jacques Rougerie Foundation - Académie des beaux-arts.

On this occasion, the Prince was awarded the Grand Medal of the Société d'encouragement au progrès (SEP) for his exceptional commitment to environmental protection and progress.

Jacques Rougerie acknowledged the Prince’s immense contribution to the defense of the planet, emphasizing that his efforts resonate deeply with the values of the Foundation: combining Arts, Sciences, Technologies, and Nature to build the future of our civilizations.

This powerful moment embodies the alliance of vision and action for a sustainable future, led by two pioneers who share the same pursuit of progress.

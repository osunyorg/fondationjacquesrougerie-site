GRAND PRIZE EX AEQUO FOR ARCHITECTURE AND INNOVATION RELATED TO CLIMATE AND RISING SEA LEVELS
Mallorquín Purification Park

Inspired by Zenú crops, the project proposes water management of 36 hectares for coastal cities in the Caribbean. It combines natural solutions with advanced technologies to prevent floods, purify water, protect ecosystems and create public spaces. Team:  Alejandro Vargas Marulanda / David Mesa Arbelaez Country:  Colombia

Blow

An urban planning model for Manoka, aimed at reducing poverty while restoring the environment in the face of climate change impacts. This plan creates an adapted ecosystem by preserving mangroves and alleviating pressure on local resources. Team: Ydriss-junior Ewoukem Nde / Doulan'ni Tizi Toumbarou Country: Cameroon

GRAND PRIZE FOR ARCHITECTURE AND INNOVATION FOR THE SEA
Sedna

Wave energy supporting energy transition, independence, and fishing for Arctic peoples. Team: Constance Fromentin, Juliette Amisse, Marine Bouyssonie, Maé Litou, Etienne Tijou, Marine Tacquet Country: France

GRAND PRIZE EX AEQUO FOR ARCHITECTURE AND INNOVATION FOR SPACE
Avatar Mission

A space mission aimed at studying Jupiter's moons, Europa and Ganymede, with a manned spacecraft. It uses robotic avatars to perform dangerous tasks on these icy worlds, combining human exploration with advanced technologies. Team: Sharipov Dilshod Country: Tajikistan

Mama Quilla

A biomimetic habitat, inspired by mammalian skin for protection and regulation, with an ecosystemic interior adapted to a low-gravity celestial body. The simple technologies used, such as natural air management and wall porosity, are applicable to various types of space transportation, particularly for lunar missions. Team: Louisette, Fanjasoa Rasoloniaina / Olivier Walter Country: France

SPECIAL LAB AWARD FOR ARCHITECTURE AND INNOVATION
I.L.S.A. – Innovative Laboratory for Sustainability and Analysis

A self-sustaining marine platform dedicated to ecosystem research and predictive analysis of climate change. Team: Patrizion Maria Puppo Country: Italy

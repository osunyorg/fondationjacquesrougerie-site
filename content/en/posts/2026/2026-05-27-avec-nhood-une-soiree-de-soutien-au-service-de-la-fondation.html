At the Académie des beaux-arts, in the presence of its Permanent Secretary Laurent Petitgirard, the Jacques Rougerie Foundation – Académie des beaux-arts brought together entrepreneurs, business leaders, decision-makers, and committed personalities for a fundraising dinner organized with the support of NHOOD and its ecosystem. The evening provided an opportunity to present the Foundation’s initiatives in support of youth, architecture, innovation, and the living environments of today and tomorrow. It also highlighted the Foundation’s international ambitions while strengthening a community of partners united by a shared conviction: empowering young creators from around the world to imagine and build concrete responses to the major challenges of the 21st century. Through its International Architecture and Innovation Competition, its global network of Junior Ambassadors spanning five continents, and the many projects it supports, the Foundation continues to pursue its mission by bringing together arts, sciences, technologies, and nature in service of territories and future generations. The Foundation extends its warmest thanks to Antoine Grolin, whose longstanding commitment and renewed trust provide invaluable support for its development. It also expresses its sincere gratitude to the teams of NHOOD, as well as to partners across its ecosystem, including Ceetrus and Projex, for their commitment and their contribution to the success of an evening dedicated to philanthropy, innovation, and collective engagement.

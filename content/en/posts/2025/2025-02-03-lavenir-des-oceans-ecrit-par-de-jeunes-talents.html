📖🪶 Jacques Rougerie had the pleasure of serving on the jury for “Writing the Future of the Oceans,” organized by the Labo des histoires as part of the Year of the Sea. During this event, more than 200 stories created by young talents aged 15 to 25 were read, from which 26 were selected for their hope and bold vision for the future of our oceans and coastlines.

This initiative fully aligns with the Foundation’s mission: to highlight the creativity and commitment of new generations in addressing global challenges.

Kudos to the Labo des histoires teams and all the partners involved in providing a platform for these young voices who are imagining the future!

Exhibition « Deep Blue - The revealed ocean »

The Franciscans, Deauville - until September 21, 2025

This summer, dive into the fascinating world of the ocean through the exhibition Deep blue - The ocean revealed.

Betweenart, science and imagination, the journey takes you from the first marine explorations to the major contemporary ecological issues. Among the works presented:

  • dream worlds of Odilon Redon,
  • visionary scaphandres inspired by Jules Verne,
  • the striking installations of the collective Niceaunties,
  • and underwater village designed in 1973 by the oceanographer architect Jacques Rougerie, poetic and daring manifesto of a habitat in symbiosis with the ocean.

A true aesthetic and committed experience, the exhibition questions our fascination with abysses, our thirst for discoveries and the urgency of preserving the living.
Labelled Sea in common It is intended for families as well as for art and ocean enthusiasts.

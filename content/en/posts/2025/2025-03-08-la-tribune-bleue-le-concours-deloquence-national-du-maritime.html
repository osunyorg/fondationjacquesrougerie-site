Before an exceptional jury, including Jacques ROUGERIE who had the pleasure of participating, 15 finalists stirred an enthusiastic audience with their speeches about the sea. ‚ú®

Congratulations to the winners Robin Dubreuil, Mahaba Al Qahwachi, and Malo Billecocq, as well as to all the finalists! üëè

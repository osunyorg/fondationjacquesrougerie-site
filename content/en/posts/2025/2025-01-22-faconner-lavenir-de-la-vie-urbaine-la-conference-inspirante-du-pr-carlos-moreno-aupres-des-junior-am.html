We extend our most heartfelt gratitude to Carlos Moreno, a distinguished Ambassador of the Foundation, for the captivating conference he presented to the participants of our Junior Ambassador program, which brings together young talents from around the globe. Thank you once again, Carlos, for sharing your expertise and passion!

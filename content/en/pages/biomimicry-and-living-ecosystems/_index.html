Nature, with its 3.8 billion years of evolution, allows us to explore new avenues of innovation. An infinite source of inspiration, it designs and manufactures sustainably, creating the most beautiful forms and offering the best materials. A true multidisciplinary adventure, the Foundation has placed biomimicry at the heart of its project to imagine the art and architecture of tomorrow, based on the preservation of our ecosystems and biodiversity.

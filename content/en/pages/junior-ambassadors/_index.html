Welcome to the very first edition of Sustainable Horizon , the official magazine of the Junior Ambassadors of the Jacques Rougerie Foundation .
This publication aims to highlight the vision, actions, and impact of a new generation of architects and scientists committed to building a more sustainable future.
As the world faces major challenges - climate change, rising sea levels, rapid urbanization, and space exploration - this magazine serves as a platform for solutions, innovation, and leadership, driven by young changemakers from around the globe.
Vision & Objectives
More than just a collection of articles, Sustainable Horizon is a statement of intent:


To showcase the contributions of the Junior Ambassadors in their respective regions and fields;


To promote pioneering ideas, projects, and innovations in sustainability, biomimetic design, and ecological resilience;


To build a strong network of thinkers and practitioners capable of influencing policy and inspiring action.


This magazine is intended as a tool for empowerment, knowledge sharing, and global impact - helping the Ambassadors establish themselves as key players in shaping the transformation of our world.

Message of the Junior Ambassadors Program's Director
The Junior Ambassador Program of the Fondation Jacques Rougerie unites exceptional young talents from across the globe, individuals from diverse cultural, design, and technical backgrounds brought together under Mr. Jacques Rougerie’s vision of inspiration, creativity, and hope. Though we speak different languages, we share one voice: to tackle the planet’s most urgent challenges and inspire younger generations to safeguard our blue marble. Our journey is guided by innovation, from ocean preservation and coastal resilience to visionary explorations of outer space. We strive to protect, regenerate, and celebrate the world we call home.” Mohamed Emad

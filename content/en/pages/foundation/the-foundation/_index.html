Foundation's ambitions

AWARENESS | Raise awarness about the sea and space problematics to call for action by all, and allow new relationships beetween humans and their environment. TRANSMISSION | Share knowledge and trigger vocations by promoting innovation in architecture, techniques, design, and all fields open to a responsible foresight.  ACTION | Offering to the emerging talents the possibility to imagine and build the symbiotic environment of tomorrow in order to highlight and enhance the resources of the planet.

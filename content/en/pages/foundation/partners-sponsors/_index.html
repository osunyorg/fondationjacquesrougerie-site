By supporting the Jacques Rougerie Foundation , you become an actor of a new society, with humanistic values and turned towards progress by combining Arts, Sciences, Technologies and Nature.
You offer young artists and creators the possibility to surpass themselves and realize their innovative projects by joining an international network of renowned experts.
You enable the emergence of decisive solutions to build future resilient, responsible, desirable and sustainable lifestyles at the heart of living ecosystems.
Your name will enter permanently the Academy of Fine Arts – Institut de France and become part of a global community committed to climate and environmental issues.
Let's work together to build the future of our civilizations through bold creativity and cross-disciplinary knowledge!

The Jacques Rougerie Foundation organizes galas for its partners and sponsors, bringing together the sea and space community meet for a priviliged moment. Prepared by renowned chefs, such as Gaël Orieux, a Michelin-starred chef with a passion for diving, these prestigious events invite the most eminent French or international personalities with links to the Foundation. Auctions offer unforgettable experiences: a parabolic flight with astronaut Jean-François Clervoy, a two-day visit aboard the Charles de Gaulle aircraft carrier, a day at sea with yachtsman Loïk Peyron aboard one of the world's largest trimarans, a dive aboard a scientific submarine, a flight with astronaut Jean-Loup Chrétien aboard his plane...
